## Project Overview

Earthy Soul Living needed a digital solution that would allow their established local yoga practice to transcend geographical boundaries. The challenge was to create an online platform that could maintain the intimate, personal connection of in-person classes while generating passive income through membership subscriptions.

## Key Features

### User-Friendly Content Management
- Intuitive drag-and-drop page builder
- Easy video content uploads
- Simple membership level management
- Custom page layouts and styling

### Membership Platform
- Secure subscription-based access
- Tiered membership levels
- Automated billing management
- Member engagement analytics

### Video Content Delivery
- High-quality streaming infrastructure
- Organized content library
- Progress tracking
- Downloadable resources

### Holistic Services Integration
- Online booking for private sessions
- Digital workshop registration
- Integrated astrology scheduling
- Energy work consultation booking

## Results and Impact

The platform has transformed Earthy Soul Living's business model:

- **Community**: Maintained strong connections with relocating clients
- **Revenue**: Established consistent passive income through subscriptions
- **Reach**: Expanded client base beyond geographical limitations
- **Content**: Growing library of over 100 yoga videos
- **Empowerment**: Client can independently update and manage their website
